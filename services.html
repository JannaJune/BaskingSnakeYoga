Services

If this is your first time trying yoga or seeking out safe ways to move through poses, this is a great place to start. I love, love, LOVE, to help people break down poses and work out safe sequencing. No matter where you are in your journey, no matter how much or little you have done in yoga, you can always start where you are. The only requirement is allowing yourself to become comfortable with being uncomfortable. This is where 'get comfortable in your own skin' comes in. We can work together so that your yoga journey is a safe and fun one.
